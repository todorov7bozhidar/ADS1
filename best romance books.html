The best romance books  in my opinion

 

 

1)Powerless
  • Hidden identity / secret powers
  • Enemies-to-lovers
  • Survival competition / deadly trials
  • “I’m watching you” tension
  • Forbidden attraction
  • Strong FMC without powers in a power-based world
  • Slow-burn with constant danger
2)The cruel Prince
  • Enemies-to-lovers (true enemies)
  • Bully romance (fantasy setting)
  • Political intrigue / court manipulation
  • Mortal in a fae world
  • Power-hungry heroine
  • Hate-to-respect-to-love
  • Betrayal & mind games
3)Fourth wing
  • Enemies-to-lovers (true enemies)
  • Bully romance (fantasy setting)
  • Political intrigue / court manipulation
  • Mortal in a fae world
  • Power-hungry heroine
  • Hate-to-respect-to-love
  • Betrayal & mind games
4)Divine rivals
  • Rivals-to-lovers
  • Anonymous pen pals
  • Hidden identity (emotional)
  • War-time romance
  • Slow-burn / soft yearning
  • Emotional intimacy before physical
  • “You know me but don’t know it’s me”
5)Once upon a broken heart
  • Deal with a devil / fae bargain
  • Morally gray love interest
  • Fairytale retelling vibes
  • Unpredictable magic
  • Love triangle energy (light but present)
  • “Don’t trust him… but you will”
  • Fate vs choice
6)Shatter me
  • Touch her and you die (literally)
  • Villain gets the girl
  • Love triangle
  • Broken/unstable narrator
  • Found family
  • Girl as weapon
  • Emotional healing arc
7)God of wrath
  • Dark obsession / stalker romance
  • Possessive antihero
  • Morally black MMC
  • Toxic relationship dynamics
  • Fear → desire
  • “You’re mine” energy
  • Psychological intensity
8)Twisted series
  • Grumpy x sunshine
  • Fake dating
  • Brother’s best friend
  • Forbidden romance
  • Billionaire romance
  • Possessive/protective MMC
  • Trauma & emotional healing
  • Interconnected friend group
9)The love hypothesis
  • Fake dating
  • Grumpy x sunshine
  • Professor x student (grad-level, ethical tension)
  • Slow-burn
  • STEM/academic setting
  • “He falls first”
  • Awkward heroine
10)The edge of darkness triology
  • Childhood friends to lovers
  • Second chance romance
  • Dark past / trauma bonding
  • “We were everything once”
  • Crime / dangerous world
  • Emotional angst & heartbreak
  • Reunited lovers
11)The royals triology
  • Rich family / elite school drama
  • Enemies-to-lovers
  • Bully romance
  • Forced proximity (living together)
  • “You don’t belong here”
  • Toxic family dynamics
  • High angst + jealousy
12)The perfect spiral
  • Brother’s best friend
  • Forbidden romance
  • Sports romance (NFL)
  • Slow-burn tension
  • Overprotective family
  • Opposites attract
  • Jealousy & miscommunication
