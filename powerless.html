Powerless- by Lauren Roberts

In a kingdom where power determines your worth and weakness is punished with death, Paedyn Gray has mastered the art of invisibility—not by disappearing, but by becoming exactly what the world expects to see. Born without abilities in a society that hunts her kind, she survives by performing a lie so convincing even the most dangerous people believe it. Every glance is calculated. Every reaction controlled. Because one slip—one moment of truth—is all it would take to end her life.

But when fate pulls her into the Purging Trials, survival stops being a quiet game.

The trials are brutal, public, and unforgiving—a spectacle where the strongest rise and the weak are eliminated. Surrounded by Elites with real power, Paedyn is forced into a deadly performance where she must outthink opponents who could destroy her in seconds. Strength isn’t her weapon. Deception is.

And then there’s Prince Kai.

A weapon in human form. Trained to detect weakness. Built to destroy people exactly like her.

From the moment he notices her, everything shifts. His attention is sharp, relentless—dangerously close to uncovering the truth she’s buried her entire life. Yet the more he watches her, the more something else begins to grow. Something neither of them fully understands. Suspicion turns into fascination. Control turns into tension. And tension… into something far more dangerous.

Because in a world built on power, the greatest risk isn’t losing the trials.

It’s being seen for who you truly are.

 

Vibe: The Hunger Games × X-Men — survival, secrets, and a slow-burn that feels like a ticking time bomb.
